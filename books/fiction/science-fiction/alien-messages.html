Alien Messages: News from Distant Worlds!


by The Abbotts


Have you ever wondered how aliens think? How they live? What they want us to know? Well this book will help you to understand more about the Pleiadians (Starseeds), Sirians, Orions, Arcturans, Andromedans to name but a few incredible Off-Planet races! There is also fascinating information from the Greys, Lizzies and the Ashtar Command! For anyone interested in aliens, this is the book for you!
